faq

quick answers

what is a “frammento”?
a short, expressive text fragment — optionally with an image — designed for calm creation and reading.
how does privacy work?
set your account to private, use content warnings, and control who can follow you. firestore security rules enforce ownership and visibility.
is eepy accessible?
yes. we support dynamic type, clear labels, grouped elements, and respect reduced motion and transparency preferences.
